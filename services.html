What We Do

Our Services

From concept to commissioning — a full spectrum of construction services delivered with the discipline Ottawa's most demanding projects require.

01

Institutional Construction

Hospitals, government buildings, courthouses, civic centres, schools, and community facilities — institutions demand a builder who understands public accountability, strict regulatory compliance, and the scrutiny of public clients. Graebeck has delivered some of the National Capital Region's most high-profile public works projects.

We are experienced with federal procurement frameworks, Infrastructure Ontario processes, and public-private partnership models. Our teams are security-cleared where required and understand the unique constraints of building while facilities remain operational.

  • Hospitals & Health-Care Facilities
  • Federal & Municipal Government Buildings
  • Courts & Justice Facilities
  • Education & Community Centres
  • Emergency Services (Fire, Police, EMS)
See Institutional Projects

02

Commercial Build-Out

Office towers, retail complexes, mixed-use podiums, and industrial facilities — commercial clients need a contractor who respects their bottom line as much as their vision. Graebeck brings iron-clad scheduling, transparent budget reporting, and a track record of on-time delivery to every commercial mandate.

We handle full core-and-shell construction, tenant coordination, and phased occupancy planning. Our value engineering process routinely identifies 5–10% in savings without compromising design quality.

  • Office & Corporate Headquarters
  • Retail & Mixed-Use Developments
  • Industrial & Warehouse Facilities
  • Hospitality & Multi-Residential Podiums
See Commercial Projects

03

Historical Restoration

Heritage buildings demand a builder who understands that the original craftsman's intent matters as much as structural integrity. Graebeck's restoration specialists work to the Standards and Guidelines for the Conservation of Historic Places in Canada, ensuring every intervention preserves, stabilizes, and honours the built heritage.

From masonry repointing and carved stone replacement to timber frame repair and heritage window restoration — our team brings technical skill and genuine reverence for historic fabric to every restoration project.

  • Masonry Cleaning, Repointing & Replacement
  • Heritage Façade Rehabilitation
  • Structural Stabilization of Historic Fabric
  • Roofing: Copper, Slate & Period Materials
  • Parks Canada Standards Compliant
See Restoration Projects

04

LEED & Green Building

Sustainability is not an add-on for Graebeck — it's embedded in our standard construction practices. We have supported LEED Certified, Silver, Gold, and Platinum projects across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors.

Our team maintains detailed waste diversion logs, sources local and recycled materials, and coordinates with mechanical and electrical sub-trades to maximize energy performance credits. We can serve as your LEED compliance coordinator from pre-design through final certification submission.

  • LEED Certification Support & Documentation
  • Construction Waste Diversion Management
  • Low-VOC & Recycled Material Sourcing
  • Net-Zero Ready Envelope Detailing

05

Project Management

For owners who require independent, expert oversight of their construction program, Graebeck offers dedicated project management services. We act as your owner's representative — protecting your interests, managing your schedule, and holding your budget accountable at every milestone.

  • Schedule Development & Monitoring
  • Sub-Trade Tender & Procurement
  • RFI & Submittal Management
  • Cost Reporting & Change Order Control
  • Deficiency & Closeout Management

06

Renovation & Retrofit

Extending the life and performance of your existing asset is often the smartest capital investment you can make. Graebeck specializes in occupied-building renovations, seismic retrofits, building envelope upgrades, and full interior gut-and-rebuild programs.

Our renovation teams understand how to work around active tenants and building users. Detailed noise, dust, and vibration management plans are standard on all occupied-building projects.

  • Occupied-Building Phased Renovation
  • Seismic & Structural Retrofits
  • Mechanical & Electrical Upgrades
  • Building Envelope Improvements
